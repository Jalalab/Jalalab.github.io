I'm Jalal Abedin, a final-year CSE student at North South University, Dhaka. My research sits at the intersection of adversarial machine learning, federated learning, and computer vision.

My Final Year Project — PromptTrace — investigates GCG-style adversarial suffix attacks on POS tags targeting Stable Diffusion v1.5, evaluated with ASR and Semantic Shift Rate (SemSR) metrics.

I've co-authored a paper on real-synthetic artwork classification using Vision Transformers (DaViT, MViT). Actively seeking ML-leaning Data Science roles post-graduation.
